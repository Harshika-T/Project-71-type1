What's New?

  • PE Equipments

    For our most loved PE teachers, who have been working with the same equipment for the past 20 years, our 6th, 7th, and 8th graders have raised money to buy new balls, cones, ropes, and many more things. New game equipments have also been introduced by students!

  • New lunch Menu for Vegetarians and Non-Vegetarians!

    Many of our students prefer non-vegetarian, but there are some who only eat veggies. Now for those students we would like to introduce a new lunch menu with the most favorite dishes, such as pizza, veg burger, veggie nuggets, and alot more. At the same time we would like to introduce a new lunch menu for our non-vegetarians like beef burger, chicken burger,chicken pizza, beans, and chicken roll, and nuggets with fries.
